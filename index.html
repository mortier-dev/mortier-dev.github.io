Mortier: File Retention You Can Prove.

Mortier is a GDPR-friendly file retention manager that helps organizations automatically retain, archive, and delete files according to policy — while generating audit-ready records that stand up in reviews and investigations.

Mortier will launch soon on mortier.dev. The secure dashboard will live at dash.mortier.dev.

What Mortier will do for you

Policy-based retention

Automate rules like “archive after 2 years” or “delete after 7 years”.

Immutable audit logs

Evidence of every action — who, when, and why.

Legal holds

Pause deletion instantly for sensitive or disputed records.

Secure archiving

Encrypted, versioned storage with full traceability.

Location-aware storage

Choose data regions to support locality expectations under GDPR.

Compliance-first design

Built specifically around retention and governance workflows.

On the roadmap

API & integrations

Connect HR, legal and storage platforms seamlessly.

Scheduled compliance reports

Automated monthly/quarterly reporting for leadership.

Role-based permissions

Separate admin, operator, and auditor access securely.

Flexible subscription plans

Simple tiers based on storage, features, and organizations.

⚠️ Mortier is currently under active development. Features may evolve — we’re building transparently and with feedback from early users.
